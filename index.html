We offer a wide range of plastering services, including:

  • Interior Plastering: From flawless smooth finishes to intricate decorative plasterwork, we have the expertise to transform your interiors into stunning works of art. Whether you need plastering for walls, ceilings, arches, or niches, we have the skills to create beautiful and durable surfaces.
  • Exterior Plastering: Our exterior plastering services are designed to enhance the curb appeal of your property while providing long-lasting protection. From traditional rendering to modern acrylic finishes, we offer a range of options to suit your aesthetic preferences and climate requirements.
  • Plaster Repair and Restoration: If your plaster surfaces have suffered from cracks, water damage, or general wear and tear, our team can restore them to their former glory. We have the knowledge and techniques to repair and rejuvenate damaged plaster, ensuring a seamless and durable finish.
  • Plaster Mouldings: Add elegance and sophistication to your space with our bespoke plaster mouldings. From cornices and ceiling roses to decorative columns and architraves, we can create custom plaster mouldings that reflect your style and elevate the overall design of your home or business.
